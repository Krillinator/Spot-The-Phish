What is phishing?

Phishing is when attackers pretend to be someone you trust to steal information.

Social engineering

Attacks succeed by exploiting human behavior such as trust, urgency, and authority.

Why this matters

Understanding phishing helps identify threat actors and their methods — not just individual attacks.

Quick checks

  • Was I expecting this message?
  • Does it create urgency or pressure?
  • Is the sender/domain actually legitimate?
  • Is it asking me to log in, pay, or share information?
  • Does the link hide anything (shorteners, AMP, fragments)?
  • Can I verify this without using the link or number provided?
  • If unsure.. don’t click. Verify independently.
